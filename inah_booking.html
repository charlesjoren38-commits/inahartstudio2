Tattoo Aftercare Guide
Inah Art Studio · Alicia, Bohol

How you care for your tattoo in the first few weeks determines how it looks for life. Follow these steps.

First 24 Hours
1Keep the wrap on for 2 to 4 hours after your session.
2Wash gently with unscented antibacterial soap and lukewarm water using clean hands only.
3Pat dry with a clean paper towel. Never rub.
4Apply a thin layer of unscented lotion (Lubriderm, Cetaphil, or similar).
Days 2 to 14
1Moisturize 2 to 3 times daily.
2No soaking — no pools, ocean, or baths. Quick showers only.
3Don't pick or scratch when it starts to peel. Let it flake naturally.
4Avoid tight clothing over the tattoo area.
Long-term
1SPF 30+ whenever the tattoo is exposed to sun. Keeps lines sharp for years.
2Full healing takes 2 to 4 weeks on the surface, up to 3 months deep.
3Touch-ups available after full healing. Message me to arrange.
⚠ See a doctor immediately if you notice excessive swelling, pus, or a fever.
